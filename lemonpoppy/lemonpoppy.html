LEMON POPPYSEED COOKIES

INGREDIENTS


for the cookies

  • 2 cups gluten free flour
  • ⅔ cup vegan buttery spread
  • 1 cup organic cane sugar
  • 2 tbs unsweetened applesauce
  • 2 ½ tbs fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tsp lemon zest be careful when you zest the lemon to get only the yellow, not any white!
  • 1 tbs poppy seeds

for the glaze

  • 1 ½ cups confectioner's sugar
  • 1-2 tbs lemon juice or water

INSTRUCTIONS

make the cookies:

  1. In a large bowl, beat the buttery spread, sugar, applesauce, and lemon juice on medium speed until light and fluffy.
  2. Add the gluten free flour blend and lemon zest, mix to combine. Add the poppy seeds and mix. (If the dough seems too wet, add a bit more flour. Texture can vary with different gluten free flour blends).
  3. Divide the dough into two parts, and shape into discs. Wrap the discs with plastic wrap and refrigerate for 2 hours, or freeze for about 30 minutes.
  4. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Line two cookie sheets with parchment paper.
  5. Lightly flour a rolling pin and rolling surface with gluten free flour. Place the dough on the surface and roll to about a ¼ inch thickness. (If the dough is too cold and stiff, you may have to let it warm up just a little bit before rolling.
  6. Bake at 350 for 12a-14 minutes.
  7. Cool for a few minutes on the cookie sheets, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.

Make the glaze:

  1. Sift the confectioner's sugar into a mixing bowl.
  2. Add the lemon juice, or water, depending on if you want a lemony glaze, or just a sugary glaze, and whisk. If the glaze is too thick, add more liquid.
  3. Drizzle glaze over cooled cookies.

*note: you can use regular all-purpose flour or white spelt flour if you don't need these to be gluten free.
